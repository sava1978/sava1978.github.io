Introduction

This is a collection of my favourite websites on NeoCities and on the web in general.

melonking.net

Man, this must be the most beautiful website I have ever seen. Everything is perfect there. The music, the visuals, the uncountable amount of easter eggs, the ASCII art - everything is just incredible. Please check it out if you love the web 1.0 aesthetic.

RANFREN

It's a site for a webcomic called "Randal's Friends" (RANFREN). Although I haven't read the actual comic, the art is very cool. You can see the dedication that the author has for his work. He even made shimejis for his characters!

Tholman's cursor effects

There are a lot of cool cursor effects here. They are written in JS and they are even mobile-friendly! One of them is used on this site.

GIFYPET

A very cool website that allow you to create a virtual pet on your website. It's like a Tamagotchi, but way cooler! It's also from the same guy, that created melonking.net. Also I have hidden my own GIFYPET on my own website. Good luck finding it :)

Ozwomp Online

Another webpage from MelonKing! This one is also amazing. It's like a retro 3D world entirely within your browser. It says that it's multiplayer, but I am not sure how true it is. Anyway, it's cool AF!

That's it for now! This list is by no way complete and I will add to it in the future, but it'll do for now.
